Wearable Antenna with FSS for SAR Reduction

A compact 2.4 GHz ISM band wearable microstrip antenna integrated with a Frequency Selective Surface (FSS) designed and simulated using CST Studio Suite 2024. The proposed antenna operates in the 2.42–2.45 GHz range and is optimized for Wireless Body Area Network (WBAN) and wearable healthcare applications.

The antenna is fabricated on a flexible polyamide substrate and enhanced with a 3×3 FSS reflector structure to suppress backward radiation toward the human body. A realistic multilayer human phantom consisting of skin, fat, muscle, and bone was used for SAR evaluation.
